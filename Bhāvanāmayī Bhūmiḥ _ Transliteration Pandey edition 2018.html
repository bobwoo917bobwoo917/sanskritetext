This file is a transcription (from devanāgarī, with original layout) of the Bhāvanāmayī Bhūmiḥ from Jagdishwar Pandey’s (2018) edition of the Yogācārabhūmi (part II), published by the K.P. Jayaswal Research Institute, Patna. To facilitate smoother reading of the text, some changes (marked in italics) to the edition for obvious typographical errors and misreadings of the text are given in notes (under [B:]), but they are by no means systematic and comprehensive, as this is not the place to present a “new” edition of the text.

 

The reason for sharing this file is mainly twofold: 1. it is unknown that when Sugawara’s critical edition (based on his thesis at Hamburg) of the text will be published (if it will be published at all); and 2. Pandey’s edition of the Yogācārabhūmi has unfortunately remained largely unknown to the scholarly world since 7+ years of its publication.

 

Although Pandey’s edition –– or rather, his transcription of the YoBh-Ms with some editorial efforts –– suffers from various drawbacks, e.g. it contains numerous errors (both typographical and textual-critical) and has but minimal attempts to rectify errors of the manuscript (not to mention that the editor has made no use of the ŚrBh-Ms), the hard work of making available e.g. the Śrutamayī, Cintāmayī and Bhāvanāmayī Bhūmiḥ into a modern edition (albeit an edition which has a huge room for improvement) should be recognized and warmly welcomed. Teams/scholars from e.g. Germany, Korea and Japan who are working on the critical edition of these three Bhūmis should find this a useful reference.
